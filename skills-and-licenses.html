Certifications and Licenses

IMI level 3 award in Automotive refrigerant handling

August 2023 to Present

A professional qualification that certifies competence in the safe handling and management of automotive refrigerants, in compliance with current environmental legislation. This award covers key areas including refrigerant recovery, recycling, system evacuation, recharging, and leak detection, with a strong emphasis on environmental responsibility and health and safety procedures. Successfully completing this qualification demonstrates both technical proficiency and legal compliance for working on vehicle air conditioning systems.

IMI level 1 award in Electric/Hybrid Vehicle awareness

August 2023 to Present

An introductory qualification providing a fundamental understanding of electric and hybrid vehicle technology. This award covers key topics including vehicle types, basic operating principles, potential hazards, and safety precautions when working around electric/hybrid systems. Designed for individuals entering the industry or working near these vehicles, it establishes essential awareness of the technologies and the importance of safe practices in line with current industry standards.
